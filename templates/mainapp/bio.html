Bio
Always seeking to expand his horizons, New Jersey-born countertenor Eric Schlossberg is a man of many talents. His recent performance record has been a display of his prowess in both early and new music; in his 2014/2015 season, Eric sang the title role in Ravel's L'enfant et ls Sortilè, Nerone in L'incoronazione di Poppea, Tolomeo in Giulio Cesare, Cirilla in Cavalli's Gli Amori d'Apollo e di Dafne, and Damon in Acis and Galatea.

More recently Eric began his foray into the world of Chinese art song with the prestigious iSING! International Young Artists Festival. During this festival, he performed in Mandarin with the Shanghai Philharmonic Orchestra and the Shanghai Symphony Orchestra in various cities throughout China.

In 2016, Eric will be performing his first US premiere of a new opera with the Thompson Street Opera Company in Louisville, KY.
